# JUN International Research Index

## Editorial rule

The museum records an event date from the video title or description when one is stated. If neither contains a date, the publication date is used and explicitly identified as an upload date. Archive capture dates prove that a page existed by that point; they do not automatically become the event date.

Every timeline claim should retain a public URL and one of these evidence labels: primary record, contemporary press, official chart, archived community page, or community video. The private JUN chat is a discovery index only.

## Recovered meeting records

- **Russia, 10 December 2008:** `1/4 FIRST RUSSIAN JUMPSTYLE LEAGUE | PREVIEW`, with the publication date used because the surviving metadata does not state a separate event day. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bEzrZSwCEnM

- **Poland, archived 16 April 2009:** JumpstylePolska preserves dedicated `Zloty` and national league forum sections. https://web.archive.org/web/20090416222333/http://www.jumpstylepolska.pl/
- **United States, 2013:** the event title identifies the sixth annual Los Angeles Jump Meeting as a 2013 event; the documentary upload is dated 18 January 2014. https://web.archive.org/web/20140214012333/http://jumpstyle.com.br/2014/02/6th-annual-los-angeles-jump-meeting-2013/
